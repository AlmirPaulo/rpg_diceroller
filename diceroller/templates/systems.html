What is an RPG System?

Basically, it's a certain set of rules to play RPG. There are a lot of classical RPG systems out there, like D&D, Storytelling, GURPS and so on. But maybe you would like an easier system to play an online RPG. Here I have some suggestion of free and simple systems for you.

  • Microlite20

    Inspired in D&D, but with simple rules. A lot of suplements made by fans. This is the "Purest Essence" version. Despite of the name, it's pretty complete.

  • Lasers and Feelings

    A minimalistic Sci-Fi RPG.

  • Swords and Scrolls

    A "Lasers and Feelings" hack to play Fantasy themed games.

  • Cthulhu Dark

    A simple system to play investigative horror scenarios. Inpired on the so called Cthulhu mythos by H.P. Lovecraft.

  • Wushu RPG

    I very interesting RPG to play games with a lot of action scenes. Inspired on Kung-Fu movies. A system when you roleplay first and then roll the dices.
